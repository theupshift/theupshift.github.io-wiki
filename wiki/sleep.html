Sleep

Sleep might be one of inconvenient but necessary flaw in our biology, It's thus important to look for ways to reduce the need for it.

Guide

  • Avoid blue light 1-2 hours before bed
  • Avoid caffeine 4-6 hours before bed
  • Take cold showers before sleeping
  • Sleep at the same time every day

Tools

To minimise the effects of blue light, I use red light overlays on my laptop (Redshift) and phone (Twilight App). I only turn them off when working on activities that involve colour like web design and photo editing.
