Log is a time-tracker, inspired by similar systems, designed for single-tasking: start a log, focus on your task, and then end it when you're done. It is available here as an Electron app for Linux and macOS.

Commands are entered via a console. The app has a built-in Guide that contains a list of commands and their usage.
