Misc
  • 90-90 rule: The first 90% of the code takes 10% of the time. The remaining 10% takes the other 90% of the time.
  • Brook's law: Adding manpower to a late software project makes it later.
  • Hofstadter's law: It always takes longer than you expect, even when you take into account Hofstadter's Law.
  • Knuth's optimisation principle: Premature optimisation is the root of all evil.
  • Murphy's law: Anything that can go wrong will go wrong.
  • Pareto principle: 80% of consequences stem from 20% of causes.
  • Parkinson's law: Work expands so as to fill the time available for its completion.
  • Robustness principle: Be conservative in what you send, be liberal in what you accept.
  • Wirth's law: Software gets slower faster than hardware gets faster.
