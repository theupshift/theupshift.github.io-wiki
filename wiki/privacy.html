Guidelines

  • Be mindful of what you share
  • Be mindful of where you share it
  • Be mindful of who you share it with

Computers

  • Install Linux. Don't use Windows.
  • Encrypt your disk
  • Cover your webcam and muffle your microphone; or remove them completely if possible.

Phones

Unfortunately I'm stuck with the ubiquitous Android ecosystem. If you own an Android device like me, you can “unGoogle” it by flashing a custom ROM or, at the very least, disabling Google apps and services.

Web browsing

  • Use Firefox.
  • Never log in on public Wi-Fi networks.
  • Install an ad blocker.
  • Use temporary email services like 10 Minute Mail if necessary.
  • Search with DuckDuckGo.

Social media

Since 2017, I've deleted my Facebook and Instagram accounts. I'm currently in the process of deleting my Google account; it's not as easy as it sounds.

  • Ditch Facebook, Instagram, Google…
  • Remove metadata from files before sharing.

Current state

I'm currently using the following tools and services. These are the ones I recommend:

  • Browser: Firefox
  • E-mail: ProtonMail
  • Social: Mastodon
  • Metadata remover: mat

These are services I'm using that I don't fully advocate for and may ditch them in the near future:

  • Duolingo: Unfortunately uses Facebook SDK.
  • GitHub: It's still fine so far but I don't trust Microsoft
  • Google: Slowly being purged from my life
  • Outlook: Eww. The university created Microsoft accounts for every student so I'm stuck with this until I graduate.
  • Twitter: Annoying but it's unfortunately my main means of keeping in touch with friends ever since ditching Facebook.
  • VSCO: It's a great alternative to Instagram. I don't think I've ever had or heard of privacy issues with VSCO so far.
