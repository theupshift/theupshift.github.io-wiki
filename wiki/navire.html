Navire is a Victorian-era Linux laptop with 2GB of RAM and a 1.10 GHz processor. This is what I primarily use when working on projects. Dotfiles are available on GitHub.

Usage

When I work, I make it a point to work offline to minimise distractions and maximise focus. I only go online if I need to look something up, download something, or share something on the Internet.

  • No idling in front of the computer
  • Focus on one task at a time
  • Only go online if necessary
