Attic

The Ath (Athenaeum) is a personal wiki housing information about me and what I do. This was created to chronicle my projects and compile knowledge relevant to the life I strive to live. I hope this also inspire others in some way.

Structure

The Ath is built on top of stripped down variants of Neauoire's Oscean wiki engine and Indental format. Pages are generated from a flat-file database.

There are five page types — home, portal, project, commonplace, and status. Portals contain indexes of pages under their category. Project pages contain information about a project, including summaries from my time-tracking data. Commonplace pages contain unstructured notes. The Status page details the wiki's status — completion of documentation, missing elements, etc.

Design

Inspired by Low-Tech Magazine's solar site, the Ath is designed to be simple with only the elements essential for a pure reading experience. Much of its CSS is dedicated to legibility rather than decor. This keeps the site light and fast.

There are no ads, tracking scripts, or popups. The only JavaScript code that runs on this site are a couple of scripts that handle search and the 404 router.
