Workshop

Rabbit is an experiment in "subtle technology". When it is inactive, Rabbit is just a blank white canvas. But hidden within it is an LED matrix and the same Raspberry Pi Zero W that used to power Holly.

Applications

I'm still thinking of ways to utilise the 8x4 RGB LED matrix. As of now, I've mostly used Rabbit to display a binary clock, but there may more potential applications:

  • Weather indicator
  • Calendar
  • Server status indicator
  • Music visualiser

I will be writing a small library of Unicorn scripts sometime in early 2019. Stay tuned.
