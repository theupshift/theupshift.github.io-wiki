Rabbit

An experiment in “subtle technology”. An 8×4 RGB LED matrix powered by RPi Zero W is hidden beneath the canvas. When inactive, it's just a blank white square.

I'm still thinking of ways to utilise the LED matrix. I currently use it as a binary clock, but there may be more potential applications:

  • Weather indicator
  • Calendar
  • Server status indicator
  • Music visualiser
