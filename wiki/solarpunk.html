Solarpunk

Solarpunk envisions an optimistic future of sustainable civilisations — a green world powered by clean renewable energy, its inhabitants in harmony with the environment.

Solarpunk is a rebellion against the structural pessimism in our late visions of how the future will be.

— Connor Owens, What is Solarpunk?
