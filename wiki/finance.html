Notes

A good life is not about living within your means, it's about living below your means.

— Austin Kleon, Keep your overhead low

I'm currently not using any tool to manage finances. I once built a simple personal finance manager but I ended up curbing my spending at the same time to the point where I had very little use for it.

Guidelines

  • Spend less than you earn
  • Set up sources of passive income
  • Borrow or seek DIY alternatives; buy as a last resort
  • Avoid disposable or packaged items—see zero waste
