Productivity

To many humans, productivity is a major key point in life.As all others, I'm also into obsessing over how I can be more efficient at getting things done so I can get more done later on.

The best way to get more done is to do less. Eliminate the unnecessary from your list of priorities and focus on what you really have to do.

改善

Kaizen means “improvement” in Japanese and is also a strategy of constant, incremental improvements — 1% better every day.

Work offline

Working while disconnected from the Internet is much more effective as it helps you avoid distractions. I only go online if I need to research, download, or share something.
