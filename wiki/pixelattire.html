Atelier

Pixelattire is a daily pixel art project where I document what I wear every day throughout 2018. All art is created using GIMP and is posted on a Tumblr page. The custom lightweight Tumblr theme used is available here.

Future

I plan on continuing this project next year perhaps with a system that generates pixel art from text logs.
