MONOCAL is a calendrical system created by Ian J. Battaglia. It's a modified version of the International Fixed Calendar.

Mechanics

A year consists of 13 months (each consisting of 28 days) and a Year Day, plus an additional Leap Day on leap years. In MONOCAL, the Year Day is referred to as Chomsky Day in honour of Noam Chomsky.

  2. Dyad
