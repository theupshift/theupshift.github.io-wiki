The Aequirys is a Mithvaerian calendar.

Mechanics

The Aequirys is based on the IFC°. A year is 13 months and a Year Day (plus a Leap Day on leap years). Each month is 28 days long. The months are simply denoted by the first 13 letters of the English alphabet.
