Mors is a script written in JavaScript that calculates estimated statistical information regarding death.

Statistics

  • NLD: Number of days lived
  • PRO: % progress till EDD*
  • ETR: Estimated time remaining*
  • EDD: Estimated date of death*

* Assuming life expectancy of 70 Earth years
