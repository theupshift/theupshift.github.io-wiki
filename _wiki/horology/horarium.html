The Horarium contains a set of instructions to measure Time

Statistics

  • ETR: Estimated time remaining*
  • EDD: Estimated date of death*
  • PRO: % progress till EDD*
  • Forecast: Probability of death

* Assuming life expectancy of 70 Earth years
