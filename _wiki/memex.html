The Memex is a personal wiki about my oeuvre, research, and general existence.

Build

The Memex is built with Jekyll° and GitHub Pages°. Information herein is written and stored in _wiki and _data. Its look and layout was built with {{ site.data.dir.macian }}.

Inspiration

My friend Devine Lu Linvega°, in his talk “The Frameworks for Mystics°” at A MAZE° in Berlin (2015), brought up the idea of a website that contains everything an individual knows so that it knows enough to predict what that person should know. It's a very interesting idea that I would love to continue to explore.

The term memex comes from Vanner Bush's° 1945 The Atlantic Monthly article “As We May Think°.” The memex is described as “a device in which an individual stores all his books, records, and communications, and which is mechanized so that it may be consulted with exceeding speed and flexibility” and an “enlarged intimate supplement to memory.”
