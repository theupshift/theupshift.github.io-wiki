The Memex is a database of knowledge related to my work, research, and general existence.

Inspirations

Devine Lu Linvega, in his talk “The Frameworks for Mystics” at A MAZE in Berlin (2015), one of the things he talks about is “a website that knows everything about me.”

The term memex comes from Vanner Bush's 1945 The Atlantic Monthly article “As We May Think.” The memex is described as “a device in which an individual stores all his books, records, and communications, and which is mechanized so that it may be consulted with exceeding speed and flexibility” and a “enlarged intimate supplement to [his] memory.”
