The Log is a log and time-tracking system inspired by similar systems by Deuveir°, Neauoire° and vi°. It is available as an Electron app for Linux, macOS, and Windows which can be downloaded here°.

Usage

The Log is designed for single-tasking: start a log entry, focus on your activity, and then stop.
